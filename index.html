PRODUCT CATALOGUE

Our Signature Stone

BLACK GALAXY
GRANITE

One of the world's most prized natural stones, Black Galaxy Granite is found exclusively in Chimakurthy, Andhra Pradesh, India. As select authorised exporters, we supply this iconic material to the UAE, across the Middle East, Europe, and international markets worldwide.

Known for its strength and striking look, Black Galaxy Granite features a deep black base with natural golden specks. Each slab is unique, with biotite crystals creating its signature galaxy effect.

01
World's Only Source
Exclusively quarried in Chimakurthy, Andhra Pradesh, India — the only region in the world where this granite is found.
02
Golden Sparkle Crystals
Naturally occurring bronze and gold biotite crystals create the signature "galaxy" star-like effect.
03
Premium Grade Export
We supply high-quality Grade A slabs, tiles, and custom sizes, exporting directly to the UAE, the Middle East, and clients worldwide.
