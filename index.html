Our Signature Stone

BLACK GALAXY
GRANITE

One of the world's most prized stones — Black Galaxy granite is found only in Chimakurthy, Andhra Pradesh, India. We are among the few authorised exporters of this legendary stone.

Black Galaxy Granite is valued not just for its strength but for its unique visual appeal. No two slabs are identical, as the distribution of biotite crystals varies naturally, making each installation one-of-a-kind. Its combination of elegance, durability, and celestial beauty makes it a timeless choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
